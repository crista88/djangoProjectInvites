Prices and characteristics

Big or small your event should be perfect.So, in any case we got you covered.
Either a single invite to be sent to as many you need or a group invite to be sent to
all,you will have the same functionalities to make you event unique .

  • 0.50 $/per invite

    Individual link for each invite, unlimited number of invites and access to your event planner.

  • 29.99 $/per group invite

    Unlimited number of invites, general link and access to your invite planner

  • With either of the packages you can personalize your invite as you wish and in the same time you get a countdown for the event, RSVP list management and names displayed in the invite for your guests.

  • You can modify at any moment your invite for updates, check the statistics (confirmations or rain checks for your invites) for each guest, distribution on social media and managing your confirmation on the platform and email.

  • In the platform you will get access to your own event planner, usefully to manage your expenses for the event, programming reminders and TO DO's .

  • After your event you will be able to upload pictures from your event and you and your guest will get automatically a digital photo album with the photos and messages.For that, invite them to leave you a nice good wish message when they confirm the invite in order to have the most beautifully memory to share .
